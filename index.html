About Us

"Let's Explore Yoshida Town" is a project by 3rd year students at Yoshida Junior High School in Unnan City, Shimane Prefecture, Japan, to share the charms of their hometown with the world. Enjoy these audio descriptions of local hotspots filled with rich nature and a long-lasting history.
